Work

A year after graduating from Texas Tech University -- young, single, restless, and itching for adventure -- I moved to China.

I spent a year learning Mandarin full-time and then got an MBA from the University of Hong Kong. After the MBA I became the first employee at a Shanghai-based eCommerce startup where I built the Product and Marketing functions. We were acquired in 2011.

I then moved to Mindshare, a large British media agency, where I managed a team of 15 local staff and oversaw the planning and execution of advertising campaigns for Nike, KFC, The North Face, Dyson, and other major brands in the China market.

I returned to the U.S. in 2015, completed an MA in China Studies at Johns Hopkings SAIS, and in 2017 began working in the White House on the Section 301 Investigation that sparked the "trade war" tariffs and negotiations with the PRC government.

In 2019, I joined Strider Technologies as employee #7, where I helped build the Intelligence and Product functions.

I now live in the Texas Hill Country with my wife and son (coming soon).
